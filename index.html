Our refund policy explains when requests can be reviewed, which situations may qualify, and how long support updates usually take.

Customers may request a refund when a key is invalid, already redeemed, or cannot be delivered within the announced delay. Orders should be reported quickly so the support team can verify the issue while the transaction is still fresh.

  • Invalid or unusable key after redemption
  • Duplicate code or activation error
  • Order missing after the expected delivery window

Cases that may not qualify

Refunds are usually not granted for completed orders where the key was delivered correctly and redeemed successfully. Mistakes caused by entering the wrong email, buying the wrong plan, or changing your mind after delivery can be handled individually at your discretion.

  • Customer entered incorrect contact details

  • Request submitted long after the order was fulfilled

How reviews are handled

Every claim can be reviewed manually. Customers should provide their order email, payment confirmation, and a short description of the issue. The team can then verify the status of the key and decide whether to replace it or issue a refund.

Processing time

Once approved, refunds can be processed back to the original payment method. Processing times vary depending on the provider, but most updates are sent to the customer by email within a short support window.

  • Review target: within 24 hours
